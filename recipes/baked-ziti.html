Baked Ziti

This Delicious recipe for Baked Ziti will have you, your friends and family wanting it every night of the week!

Ingredients

  • Ziti Noodles
  • Red Sauce of your choice
  • Ground Meat or Sausages of your choices
  • Shredded Cheese of your choice

Cooking time is approximately 65 minutes, but can take a little longer.

  1. First, bring your water to a rolling boil for the pasta noodles, add the noodled and reduce heat to a high medium
  2. While your noodles cook, beging browning your ground meat or cooking your sausages.
  3. Stirring your noodles often, let cook for about 7-8 minutes, and remove from heat once cooked.
    Avoid Over Cooking by checking frequently
  4. Strain noodles of water, replace in pot, mix with cooked choice meats and pasta sauce of your choice. Ensure mixed well.
  5. Set oven to 375 Degrees, transfer mixed ziti to a baking pan at least 1.5 inches deep. Spread evenly and top heavily with choice of shredded Cheese.
  6. Tent a layer of tin foil over the tray, and cook for 35-45 minutes.
  7. Remove, let sit covered for 5-10 minutes, remove tin foil and Enjoy!
